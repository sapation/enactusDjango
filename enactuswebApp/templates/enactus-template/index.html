WHAT IS ENACTUS?

Our story

ENACTUS is an international non-profit organization that bring together student, academic and business leaders who are committed to using the power of entrepreneurial action to improve the quality of life and standard of living for people in need.It has a triangular shape which comprise of student,Faculty officer and Business Advisory Board.Participating students form teams on their university campuses and apply business concepts to develop outreach projects that improve the quality of life and standard of living for people in need.
